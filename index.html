Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Electrifi gift cards. We also offer financing options through Affirm and Klarna.

How does the extended warranty work?

Our extended warranty covers mechanical and electrical failures for up to 3 years beyond the manufacturer's warranty. It includes free repairs, replacements, and 24/7 technical support. You can add it to your purchase during checkout.

Can I return opened electronics?

Yes, most opened electronics can be returned within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in like-new condition with all original packaging and accessories. Some products like earbuds and custom-configured devices may have different return policies.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes! If you find a lower price on an identical item from a qualified retailer within 14 days of purchase, we'll match it and refund you the difference plus an additional 5% of the difference.
